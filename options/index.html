Max number of pixels to process. A higher numbers means it takes longer time to process the selection.

When the product of your selection's dimensions (width × height) exceeds the configured value, the selection will be internally scaled down to have a product of pixels.

Default: 100,000

When you experience blank analyze views although the hover is still working, turn this option on.

This extension is intended to help colorblind people to better understand the colors they see on the web and to make online content more accessible to them.
